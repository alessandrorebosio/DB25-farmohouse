Welcome

Welcome to Campo Verde: a farmhouse that combines family hospitality, farm-to-table dining, swimming pool, animal activities, and sports facilities. The website allows you to explore services, check availability, and easily book online.

You can book rooms, tables, pool loungers (2-hour slots), sports fields, and register for events; registered users can manage their bookings, purchase custom packages, and leave reviews.

Each booking is confirmed by email; staff have access to a dashboard to monitor occupancy, bookings, and reviews, and to manage offers and shifts.
